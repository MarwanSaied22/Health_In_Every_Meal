Health In Every Meal - Business Plan
📋 Project Overview
Health In Every Meal is a specialized business venture dedicated to producing and selling safe sweets for individuals with lactose, gluten, and egg allergies. Our mission is to provide high-quality, delicious products while strictly preventing cross-contamination to ensure consumer safety.
🚀 The Problem
In Egypt, individuals with gluten or lactose intolerance face significant daily challenges:
*Lack of Safe Options: A market shortage of specialized brands that guarantee 100% allergy-safe products.
*Social Isolation: Difficulty participating in family and social events, leading to a sense of exclusion.
*Quality of Life: A pressing need for reliable, healthy, and tasty solutions that restore confidence and social integration.
💡 Value Proposition
We offer a 100% safe dining experience by providing:
Complete Safety: Sweets entirely free from gluten, lactose, and eggs.
Quality Assurance: Guaranteed taste and texture with zero risk of cross-contamination.
Emotional Well-being: Reducing the feeling of social deprivation and providing peace of mind for the whole family.
